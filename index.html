Hello, I am Steven 'Cho' Chaplinski.

I guess you might consider me as a wandering wonderer, a mystic, and a seeker. For more than a decade, I've been delving into the mysteries of our history. Subjects like Comparative Mythology and Religions, Cultural Anthropology and Archeology, and Human Anatomy and Physiology I and II. Once upon a time, when the Virus hadn't emerged, I taught classes on topics like Jungle Medicine, Ceremonial Setting and Beliefs, DMT Chemistry, and many more lesser known plant medicines. As a lover of mystery, I enjoy discovering unknown objects and material to ponder. However, as an abnormally normal human, I find humans on this planet are not always kind to those on the spectrum, or critical thinkers, as you might call them.


However, an immersive study into South American Jungle Medicine Leveled me up like Mario! All those experiences, 12 vision quests later, completely changed my life and my perspectives. I explore the mysteries of consciousness through meditation, breathwork, and the jungle juice known as Ayahuasca. The greatest gift so far has been my opportunity to share what I have been shown and learned from a Spirit of Nature. I'll happily be your Cosmic Companion!
